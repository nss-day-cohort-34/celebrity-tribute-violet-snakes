Introducing: Quentin Tarantino:

Quentin Tarantino has been a highly influential and recognized film figure for the past three decades.  His directorial debut "Reservoir Dogs" gave him notice, but his second film "Pulp Fiction" launched the then budding director into stardom. Tarantino is known for being a  "Video Store Graduate", in lieu of going to film school, his job as a video store clerk gave him a vast number of films to study, appreciate, and steal from... Famously saying "I steal from every single movie ever made. If people don't like that, then tough tills, don't go and see it, all right? I steal from everything. Great artists steal, they don't do homages."(Empire Magazine 1994). While Quentin Tarantino is known for being a highly peculiar individual, he is able to balance his eccentricities, he is known on set to be personable, caring, and lovable..
